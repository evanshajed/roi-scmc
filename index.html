How to Use the Calculator

The calculators work on programmed formulas depending on the variables you input. You’ll need these numbers to get started:

  • Budget weighting by tactic
  • Retail rate you pay by tactic
  • Media costs by tactic
  • Commission percentage (note in the commission by tactic calculation, you’ll need to enter it for each tactic)
  • Advertiser budget/sale value

Insert those variables and manipulate them as you like. At the bottom of the page, you can download the results as a PDF.
